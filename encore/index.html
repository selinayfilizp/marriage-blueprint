A standing reservation for your relationship

Two nights. One coach. Zero obligations to anyone but each other.

Encore is an elevated weekend for two, built around one idea: the strongest couples do not wait for something to be wrong. You arrive Friday evening, you leave Sunday afternoon, and in between there is exactly one working session, a private workshop guided by one of our instructor coaches, woven into a weekend that otherwise looks and feels like the best short holiday you have taken together.

No group circles. No sharing with strangers. Every conversation that matters happens between the two of you, behind your own door.

The shape of the weekend

Held the same way, every year. That is the point.

FridayArrive by 6pm

Check in, drop your bags, drop your phones if you like. Aperitivo and a long dinner for two. The weekend seals itself around you. No work tonight.

SaturdayThe heart of it

Morning: your private workshop, 90 focused minutes with your coach, shaped by a short questionnaire you each answered before arriving. Afternoon: massage day. Spa, sun, silence. Evening: the signature dinner, with one beautiful question on the menu.

SundayDepart by 4pm

Slow breakfast. A short closing session where the weekend becomes three commitments for the year ahead. Then the coast road home, and a standing reservation for next year if you want it.
